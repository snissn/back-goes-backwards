Ground-State Reset – 5-Minute Bilateral Routine

A single left-and-right run-through of each loop below will unwind any postural pattern and land you in the mechanically optimal “ground state.”
No decisions or tests mid-flow—just do each side exactly as written.

Rib Loop — Rebalance Rib Cage & Scapula (all standing, ~3 min)

Step Breath Count Key Cue Targeted Region
R11 / sideReach & meltRib torque balance
R21 / sideBend + chin tuckPosterior rib twist
R33 / sideHands-on-ribs inflateRib compression
R43 centralTail down, crown upGravity line, posture
R52 centralSternum glides back & upAxial front/back balance
R61 / sideMicro-spiralsResidual rib twist

How to:

  1. R1 Reciprocate the Ribs:
    • Right: Reach right arm overhead, palm in.
      Inhale 4 s—feel right flank expand.
      Exhale 6 s—let left scapula glide down and back.
    • Left: Repeat with sides reversed.
  2. R2 Discharge Posterior Twist:
    • Right: Right arm up.
      Exhale—side-bend right, chin tuck.
      Inhale—return to center.
    • Left: Repeat with sides reversed.
  3. R3 Equalise Rib Volume:
    • Right: Hands on right lower ribs.
      3 quiet breaths into palms.
    • Left: Hands on left lower ribs. Repeat.
  4. R4 Align with Gravity:
    • Stand tall, arms relaxed. For 3 slow breaths:
      Exhale—tailbone melts down.
      Inhale—crown floats up.
  5. R5 Set Axial Balance:
    • Lace fingers behind skull. For 2 breaths:
      Inhale—sternum glides back/up.
      Exhale—maintain length, no shrug.
  6. R6 Sweep Residual Twist:
    • Right sweep: Right hand on sternum, fingers left.
      Exhale—draw breastbone right, gaze follows.
      Inhale—release to center.
    • Left sweep: Left hand on sternum, fingers right.
      Exhale—draw breastbone left, gaze follows.
      Inhale—release to neutral.

Hip Loop — Rebalance Pelvis & SI Joints (all standing, ~1 min)

Step Breath Count Key Cue Targeted Region
H11 / sideWeight-shift, hemipelvis foldPelvic coupling
H21 / sideSpiral navel, counter-reachLumbar rotation
H32 / sideHands-on-hip expand/softenIliac (hip) compression

How to:

  1. H1 Reciprocate the Hemipelves:
    • Right: Weight on right foot, left foot back 10cm.
      Inhale—right hip widens.
      Exhale—tuck left sit-bone under.
    • Left: Weight on left foot, right foot back.
      Inhale—left hip widens.
      Exhale—tuck right sit-bone under.
    Tip: Keep a slight bend in the knees so the pelvis moves, not the low back.
  2. H2 Unload the Lumbar Twist:
    • Right: Weight on right foot, left foot back.
      Exhale—spiral navel right, left arm counter-reaches.
      Inhale—return to center.
    • Left: Weight on left foot, right foot back.
      Exhale—spiral navel left, right arm counter-reaches.
      Inhale—return to center.
  3. H3 Equalise Iliac Volume:
    • Right: Hands on right hip.
      Inhale—expand waist.
      Exhale—soften hip flexors.
      Repeat 2x.
    • Left: Hands on left hip.
      Inhale—expand waist.
      Exhale—soften hip flexors.
      Repeat 2x.

Skull Loop — Fine-Tune Cranio-Cervical Balance (all standing, ~40s)

Step Breath Count Key Cue Targeted Region
K11 / sideLift skull corner, jaw swingsOcciput-atlas balance
K21 / sideSpiral jaw with tongue to palateJaw-hyoid, skull base

How to:

  1. K1 Occipital Float:
    • Right: Stand tall, eyes forward.
      Inhale—lift right back corner of skull.
      Exhale—jaw swings left.
    • Left: Inhale—lift left back corner.
      Exhale—jaw swings right.
  2. K2 Jaw-Hyoid Spiral:
    • Right: Tongue up, focus right back skull.
      Exhale—spiral jaw toward lifted corner.
      Inhale—return center.
    • Left: Tongue up, focus left back skull.
      Exhale—spiral jaw toward lifted corner.
      Inhale—return center.
You’re Done – Quick Confirmation:
Take one natural inhale.
Both sides of your ribcage, pelvis, and neck should now feel balanced and move evenly.
If any area still feels restricted or uneven, repeat the entire routine from the top.
Use one full cycle daily for maintenance; up to three cycles back-to-back for stubborn patterns.
No measurements or side-decisions needed—just follow the script and let your body reset.
