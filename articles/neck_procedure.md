## A  |  Warm‑up – Posterior Rock & Roll (3 min)

1. **Seated on a block, feet hip‑width.**
2. **Inhale:** Roll pelvis backward (tailbone tucks), slide occiput backward 1–2 mm—feel the whole outer cube shorten in the posterior line.
3. **Exhale:** Roll pelvis forward to neutral, let inner cube rebound.
4. Visualise the **outer‑cube spring** running sacrum → occiput shortening each inhale.
5. 12 breaths.

## B  |  Skull Track ③ – SCM–Erb’s Point Glide (4 min)

**Purpose:** Remove the step‑change at SI16 by gliding the posterior SCM sheath under the mastoid.

| Step | Action                                                                          | Breath cue                  | Felt‑sense                           |
| ---- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------------------- | ------------------------------------ |
| 1    | Fingertip of R hand finds **SJ16** (posterior SCM, jaw angle).                  | Inhale: light pressure.     | Cool lengthening upward.             |
| 2    | On the *same inhale*, gently retract chin & tilt left ear toward L shoulder 5°. | Inhale continues.           | Line from occiput to elbow slackens. |
| 3    | **Pause** 3 s at peak inhale; imagine outer‑cube edge **Nₗ → Eₗ** normalising.  | Breath hold.                | Micro‑pulsing behind ear.            |
| 4    | **Exhale:** Release finger pressure, let SCM slide anterior.                    | Exhale carries load inward. | Warmth in LI11/SJ10.                 |

* Do **3 cycles** left side only, **5 breaths per cycle**.
* Keep pressure *minimal*—the movement, not the poke, does the work.

## C  |  Skull Track ④ – Triple‑Junction Spiral Unwind (4 min)

**Purpose:** Fuse LI–SJ–SI into a single continuous helix, eliminating phase break (**helix parameter h → 0**).

1. **Hook‑lying** (on back, knees bent).
2. **Left arm at 90° shoulder flexion, thumb pointing to ceiling.**
3. **Inhale (yang):**

   * Trace mental line **LI11→SJ10→SJ16** while rotating left thumb **externally** \~20°.
   * Occiput presses *gently* into floor.
4. **Exhale (yin):**

   * Thumb **internally** rotates back to start; visualise line **SI16→SI17→SI8** drawing tension *into* inner cube.
5. **One spiral cycle = inhale + exhale.**
6. Perform **6 cycles**, tempo 6–6 s.
7. Final inhale: keep posterior line loaded and feel elbow “float” away from ribcage.

## E  |  Hip‑Thoracic Re‑sync Flow (6 min)

> *Think of this as an SO(4) “figure‑eight” rotation reaffirming outer/inner cube coupling.*

1. **Start in Down Dog.**
2. **Inhale:** Float L leg high ∴ posterior chain full‑length; micro‑tuck chin to maintain occiput‑sacrum line.
3. **Exhale:** Step L foot to outside of L hand → drop R knee (Lizard). Inner cube rotates anterior‑inferior.
4. **Inhale:** Twist L arm skyward (LI/SJ line lengthens).
5. **Exhale:** Thread L arm under torso (SI line).
6. Return to Down Dog → switch side.
7. *Three rounds each side* (≈ 6 breaths/round).

## F  |  Elbow Decompression Anchors (2 min)

1. **Standing, back against wall, L elbow at 90°, forearm forward.**
2. **Inhale:** Posterior neck load; gently *press* olecranon into wall (5 % effort).
3. **Hold 15 s, breathe naturally.**
4. **Exhale:** Release; shake out.
5. **3 sets**.

This locks the new edge length into the lateral epicondyle & radial collateral complex.
